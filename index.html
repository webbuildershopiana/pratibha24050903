About Us

Since its establishment in 2010, Pratibha Polymers has emerged as a premier partnership firm renowned for producing an extensive array of packaging solutions, such as pouches and bags, along with high-Caliber packaging material printing services. Our industry standing is substantial, owing to our dedication to delivering products with customizable sizes, eye-catching designs, and superior quality. We maintain transparent business practices, forging strong client relationships across the country. Our unwavering focus on ethics and commitment underscores all our business and client interactions.  

We comply with all governmental regulations and industry standards, maintaining high operational standards. Our well-organized infrastructure and dedicated team ensure efficient task execution. By segmenting work into specialized units, we enhance accountability and ensure timely completion. Our carefully selected staff maintains high efficiency across all departments.

Ayush Sharma
Owner,
Pratibha Polymers

Industries We Serve 

We’ve grown into a dynamic force, collaborating with businesses across industries to redefine packaging norms.

Food & Snacks 

The food and snacks sector is a vibrant and rapidly growing industry that encompasses a wide array of products ranging from ready-to-eat meals and gourmet delicacies to everyday staples and indulgent treats.

Frozen Foods

The frozen food sector includes a vast range of products, from frozen vegetables and fruits to ready-to-cook meals, meats, seafood, and delectable desserts.

Beverages & Liquids 

The beverages & liquids sector encompasses a wide array of products, including bottled water, juices, soft drinks, alcoholic beverages, and various liquid food products like soups and sauces.

Pet Care
Products

The Pet Care products sector is a rapidly expanding industry dedicated to meeting the diverse needs of pets and their owners, encompassing items such as pet food, treats, grooming supplies, toys, and health care products.

Industrial
Products

This sector is a cornerstone of global commerce, encompassing a wide array of goods ranging from machinery parts and equipment to chemicals and raw materials used in various manufacturing processes.

Home Care
Products

The home care products sector is a vital and fast-evolving industry that encompasses a wide range of items aimed at maintaining cleanliness, hygiene, and a healthy home environment.
